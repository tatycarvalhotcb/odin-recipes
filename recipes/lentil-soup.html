Lentil Soup

Description

Delicious soup to eat in a cold day with some garlic butter toast in the side.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Step 1: I like to let the lentils soak for at least 30 minutes before, but if you don't have time, you can use it without soaking in before.
  2. Step 2: Mince the onion and garlic and fry in a pan with olive oil. Chop the carrot into small cubes and add to the pan.
  3. Step 3: Drain the water from the lentils and mix in the pan with the tomato paste and the seasoning: cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt and pepper. If you like spice, you can add some cayenne pepper powder as well.
  4. Step 4: Add 4-5 cups of water and let it cook for about 25 minutes or until the lentils are soft. You can use a hand mixer to get a creamier texture.
  5. Step 5: Serve with toast and fresh parsley on the top.
