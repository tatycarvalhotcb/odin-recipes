Banana Bread

Description

This a healthy and easy way to make delicious banana bread to eat in your breakfast or even have it for dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 5 bananas
  • 1 cup oat
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 ml canola oil
  • 1/2 cup 60% chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pint salt

Steps:

  1. Add all the ingredients, except for the chocolate chips and the baking powder, in a blender and blend until smooth consistency.
  2. Place into a bowl and gently mix the baking powder and the chocolate chips.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and sprinkle more chocolate chips in the top.
  4. Bake at 350Â°F for 60 minutes.
